Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Online Certificate Verification System (OCVS) work?
OCVS matches your certificate ID with the encrypted registry database. Once verification completes, it returns dynamic authenticity details signed by university authorities.
What is the average verification approval time?
Standard academic credential verification takes between 24 to 48 working hours from the moment documents upload to the registrar queue.
How do I share my certificate verified profile on LinkedIn?
Navigate to the details view, click the 'Share Link' option, select LinkedIn or copy the public link. You can add this URL directly inside the Licenses & Certifications profile block.
Why is my certificate verification marked as 'Rejected'?
Rejection usually occurs if uploaded metadata does not match official registrar records, signatures are illegible, or university stamps are missing. Contact support with corrections.
Can I print a physical copy of my digital certificate?
Yes! Go to the details page, click 'Print'. The custom media print styles will crop unnecessary navbar and sidebar borders and print only the high-resolution certificate canvas.
Is my credential data storage secure?
Absolutely. Your record keys are hashed and cryptographically signed. All student records conform to university privacy compliance policies.
What format are the download certificates in?
Downloads are compiled as standardized vector-embedded PDF documents, ensuring clear scaling for printing or high-definition screens.
What is the golden verification seal?
The golden seal is a dynamic visual tag rendered only on certificates currently verified by the central registrar database.
How can a recruiter verify my certificate?
Recruiters can either scan the verified QR code directly using their mobile phones or navigate to the public verification page using the copied link.
Can I edit my name or enrollment ID in profile details?
No. Fields like name and enrollment ID are locked for security once verified. If you need to make correction requests, please submit the helpdesk contact form below.
